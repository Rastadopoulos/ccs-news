Saline-aquifer CCS proposed as US data-centre decarbonisation pathway

Mirage News / ACS Energy & Fuels (Lau & Tsai) · 19 June 2026 · [North America]

Hon Chung Lau and Steve C. Tsai project US data-centre power demand quadrupling from 40 GW (2025) to 169 GW by 2030, lifting associated CO2 from 90 Mt to 404 Mt/yr if powered by unabated fossil generation. Their mapping finds 34 US states have saline-aquifer storage adequate for >100 years of injection; co-locating NGCC-plus-CCS could mitigate ~75% of data-centre emissions by 2030. Notable as one of the first peer-reviewed papers framing AI-driven load growth as a stand-alone CCS demand driver.
