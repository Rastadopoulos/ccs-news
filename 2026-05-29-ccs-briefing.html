Shell wins Dutch permit for Aramis CO₂ storage at K14-FA/FC

Industrylinqs · 28 May 2026 · [Europe]

The Ministry of Economic Affairs and Climate has granted Shell Exploration and Production B.V. and Shell Gas & Power Developments B.V. a 25-year licence to store CO₂ at two North Sea sites (K14-FA and K14-FC), among the first storage acreage to be brought under the Gasunie/EBN/Shell/TotalEnergies Aramis project, which targets 22 Mtpa of injection from 2030.

HyNet's Encyclis Protos anchor capture plant enters new construction phase

Energy Voice · 28 May 2026 · [Europe / UK]

Foundational works (continuous flight auger and driven piling) have started on schedule at the 4.4-acre Cheshire site of the Encyclis Protos energy-from-waste CCS plant, with EPC contractor Kanadevia Inova mobilised. The unit will capture roughly 370,000 tpa CO₂ for transport via Eni's Liverpool Bay pipeline to permanent subsea storage; construction and commissioning will directly support up to 500 skilled jobs, with carbon capture operations due by mid-2029.

Alberta opens public consultation on updated CCS quantification protocol

Carbon Pulse / Sendeco2 · 28 May 2026 · [North America]

Alberta is seeking public comment on proposed updates to its CCS quantification protocol that would let eligible direct-air-capture projects account for low-carbon electricity, and would clarify how removal credits and storage reversals are handled under the province's large-emitter (TIER) system — the first major refresh since the December 2024 protocol introduced "Removal Credits" and explicitly included DAC.
