Strategic Biofuels secures final Class VI Permit to Construct in Louisiana

BusinessWire · 01 Jul 2026 · [North America]

Louisiana Department of Conservation and Energy has issued Strategic Biofuels its final Class VI Permit to Construct for the Louisiana Green Fuels Project in Caldwell Parish, following the March 2026 draft. The 100 MW wood-fired power plant will permanently sequester ~1 million tonnes/yr of CO₂ into a dedicated multi-well geologic complex directly beneath the site, generating ≥1 Mt/yr of durable CDR credits. Only the third full Class VI permit issued in Louisiana and the first to a multi-well project.

California SB 614 State Fire Marshal deadline for CO₂ pipeline safety regulations falls today

Hunton / National Law Review / KPBS · Statutory milestone falling 01 Jul 2026 · [North America]

Under SB 614 (Stern, 2025), the California State Fire Marshal is required to adopt regulations governing the transportation of CO₂ by intrastate pipeline by 1 July 2026, with standards at least as protective as PHMSA's January 2025 draft federal rule. Adoption lifts the state's intrastate CO₂ pipeline moratorium and opens the door to Californian CCUS project pipelines from 2027; the rules prohibit conversion of pipelines originally built for other fluids and bar the use of previously used pipe or components.

Alberta submits West Coast pipeline proposal on 1 July deadline — Pathways CCS hub still mutually dependent

CBC News / Globe and Mail / National Observer · 30 Jun 2026 · [North America]

The Alberta government has confirmed it will meet the federal-provincial Memorandum of Understanding deadline to lodge a ~1 million bpd West Coast oil pipeline proposal with Ottawa's Major Projects Office by 1 July 2026. Under the MoU, construction of the pipeline is treated as a prerequisite for the Pathways Alliance's ~$16 billion carbon capture and storage network — targeting 16 Mtpa of oilsands emissions reductions by 2045, with a minimum 6 Mtpa CCUS in-service by 2035. Route, proponent and federal cost-sharing all remain unresolved.
