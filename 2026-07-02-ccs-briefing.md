## Projects

## Policy

- **California SB 614 State Fire Marshal deadline for CO₂ pipeline safety regulations falls today** — Under SB 614 (Stern, 2025), the California State Fire Marshal is required to adopt regulations governing the transportation of CO₂ by intrastate pipeline by 1 July 2026, with standards at least as protective as PHMSA's January 2025 draft federal rule. Adoption lifts the state's intrastate CO₂ pipeline moratorium and opens the door to Californian CCUS project pipelines from 2027; the rules prohibit conversion of pipelines originally built for other fluids and bar the use of previously used pipe or components. (Hunton / National Law Review / KPBS, statutory milestone falling 01 Jul 2026) [link](https://www.hunton.com/the-nickel-report/california-enacts-framework-for-lifting-moratorium-on-intrastate-carbon-dioxide-pipelines)
- **Alberta submits West Coast pipeline proposal on 1 July deadline — Pathways CCS hub still mutually dependent** — The Alberta government has confirmed it will meet the federal-provincial Memorandum of Understanding deadline to lodge a ~1 million bpd West Coast oil pipeline proposal with Ottawa's Major Projects Office by 1 July 2026. Under the MoU, construction of the pipeline is treated as a prerequisite for the Pathways Alliance's ~$16 billion carbon capture and storage network — targeting 16 Mtpa of oilsands emissions reductions by 2045, with a minimum 6 Mtpa CCUS in-service by 2035. Route, proponent and federal cost-sharing all remain unresolved. (CBC News / Globe and Mail / National Observer, 30 Jun 2026) [link](https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/calgary/pipeline-proposal-major-projects-deadline-9.7251077)
