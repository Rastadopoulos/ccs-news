## Policy

- **Industry groups urge Baltic states to clear the Helsinki Convention barrier to offshore CO₂ storage** — A coalition of industry stakeholders called on Baltic-bordering governments to remove obstacles to offshore CO₂ storage. Unlike OSPAR and the London Protocol, the Helsinki Convention still legally bars sub-seabed CO₂ storage in the Baltic; an independent review of its legality, environmental impact and technical feasibility is being sought. (QCIntel, 16 Jul 2026) [paywall] [link](https://www.qcintel.com/carbon/article/industry-groups-urge-action-to-enable-baltic-offshore-co2-storage-68906.html)
