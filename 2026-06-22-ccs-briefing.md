## Technology & R&D

- **Saline-aquifer CCS proposed as US data-centre decarbonisation pathway** — Hon Chung Lau and Steve C. Tsai (Energy & Fuels, ACS) project US data-centre power demand quadrupling from 40 GW (2025) to 169 GW by 2030, lifting associated CO₂ from 90 Mt to 404 Mt/yr if powered by unabated fossil generation. Their mapping finds 34 US states have saline-aquifer storage adequate for >100 years of injection; co-locating NGCC-plus-CCS could mitigate ~75% of data-centre emissions by 2030. Notable as one of the first peer-reviewed papers framing AI-driven load growth as a stand-alone CCS demand driver. (Mirage News / ACS Energy & Fuels, 19 Jun 2026) [link](https://www.miragenews.com/underground-carbon-capture-may-curb-data-center-1695256/)
