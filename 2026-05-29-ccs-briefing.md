## Policy

- Alberta opens public consultation on updated CCS quantification protocol — Alberta is seeking public comment on proposed updates to its CCS quantification protocol that would let eligible direct-air-capture projects account for low-carbon electricity, while clarifying how removal credits and storage reversals are handled under the province's large-emitter (TIER) system — the first major refresh since the December 2024 protocol introduced "Removal Credits" and explicitly included DAC. (Carbon Pulse / Sendeco2, 28 May 2026) [link](https://www.sendeco2.com/es/noticias/2026/05/28/alberta-releases-draft-update-of-ccs-quantification-protocol-for-public-comment)
