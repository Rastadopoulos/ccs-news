## Policy

- **UK offshore CO₂ transport & storage decommissioning-funding regime takes effect** — The Carbon Dioxide Transport and Storage (Financing of Costs of Offshore Decommissioning) Regulations 2026 (SI 2026/632) come into force today, 10 July 2026, implementing s.92 of the Energy Act 2023. The regime requires licensed CO₂ transport-and-storage companies to set aside a proportion of user fees in a ring-fenced offshore decommissioning fund, accruing over an expected ~25-year operating life, so that the eventual cost of decommissioning offshore CO₂ pipelines and storage infrastructure is anticipated and funded across a project's lifecycle. A notable maturation of the UK CCS regulatory framework — and directly relevant to Australia, where offshore CO₂ storage under the OPGGS Act raises the same long-tail decommissioning-liability questions. (Energy Voice / legislation.gov.uk, 10 Jul 2026) [link](https://www.energyvoice.com/all-news/600090/ccus-decommissioning-regulations-uk/)
