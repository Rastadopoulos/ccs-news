## Projects

- **California Achieves First CO₂ Injection at Carbon TerraVault I — State's Inaugural Commercial CCS Project** — California Resources Corporation (CRC) completed the first commercial CO₂ injection at Carbon TerraVault I (CTV I) at its Elk Hills Field, Kern County — California's first operational CCS project. The site holds the state's first final US EPA Class VI underground injection permits, with annual capacity of 1.46 Mt CO₂/yr and total reservoir capacity of 38 Mt. CO₂ is sourced from CRC's on-site cryogenic gas plant and stored more than one mile underground in a depleted reservoir. CRC has submitted eight additional CTV reservoirs for permitting (~352 Mt total potential) and committed >$1 million to a Community Benefits Plan for Kern County.
