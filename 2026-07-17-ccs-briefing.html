Orcadian advances Earlham & Orwell gas-to-wire concept with integrated carbon capture

Investegate / Energy-pedia · 16 Jul 2026 · [Europe/UK]

Orcadian Energy began the Assessment Phase on its 100%-owned UK North Sea licence P2680; its preferred concept is a ~200 MW offshore gas-fired power station with integrated CO₂ capture and reinjection into the Earlham reservoir, powering a co-located offshore data centre (the "Earlham Gigagrid"). The high CO₂ content of Earlham gas makes capture-and-reinject the preferred route to monetise the resource; next steps hinge on NSTA licence assignment and a Letter of No Objection.

Industry groups urge Baltic states to clear the Helsinki Convention barrier to offshore CO₂ storage

QCIntel · 16 Jul 2026 · [Europe/UK] · [paywall]

A coalition of industry stakeholders called on Baltic-bordering governments to remove obstacles to offshore CO₂ storage. Unlike OSPAR and the London Protocol, the Helsinki Convention still legally bars sub-seabed CO₂ storage in the Baltic; an independent review of its legality, environmental impact and technical feasibility is being sought.
