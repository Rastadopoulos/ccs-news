## Policy

- **EU NZIA Article 23 deadline takes effect** — 30 June 2026 is the hard date by which EU Member States must have set penalties for breaches of the CO₂ storage obligation, and the first day the 44 obligated oil-and-gas producers must report progress toward the 50 Mtpa-by-2030 target. Current trajectory is ~29 Mtpa. (Carbon Pulse, 30 Jun 2026) [link](https://carbon-pulse.com/526948/)
- **Singapore and Indonesia close to Article 6 implementation agreement** — Local media report the bilateral text is in the final stages, lining up legal scaffolding that could support cross-border CO₂ flows between Singapore emitters and Indonesia's Asri and Tangguh storage hubs. (Carbon Pulse, 30 Jun 2026) [link](https://carbon-pulse.com/526975/)
- **Spain awards €319m for cement decarbonisation under PERTE** — €200m to Cemex's Tarragona plant and €119m to Votorantim Cimentos' Toral de los Vados (El Bierzo) facility, the latter explicitly including a carbon-capture installation.
