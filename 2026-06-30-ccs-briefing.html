Alberta-Ottawa industrial carbon price reset to $140/t puts Canadian CCS pipeline at risk

CBC News · 29 Jun 2026 · [North America]

A revised federal-Alberta industrial carbon-price agreement caps the carbon tax at C$140/t and flattens the previously legislated escalator that would have reached C$170/t by 2030. Varme Energy's shovel-ready $400 million Edmonton waste-to-energy facility with CCS, contracted to process 150,000 t/yr of municipal waste from 2028 and capture >1 Mt CO₂ over its lifetime, is now at risk of cancellation this autumn; Torchlight's ~$2 billion pulp-mill CCS project at Hinton (1.6 Mt/yr) and "pretty much every" other Canadian CCS project would also be challenged at the lower price.
