## Projects

- **INPEX spuds Japan's first offshore CO₂-storage appraisal well off Chiba** — INPEX (85%) and Kanto Natural Gas Development (15%), through their Metropolitan CCS JV, have begun drilling the Kujukuri-Oki J-1 appraisal well ~5 km off Kujukuri, Chiba — the first offshore CO₂-storage appraisal well drilled in Japan. The METI/JOGMEC-backed Advanced CCS project (drilling approved 15 April) targets CO₂ from Nippon Steel's Kimitsu works and other Keiyo industrial-zone emitters, with an initial 1.3 Mtpa injection rate scalable to 5 Mtpa, commercial storage in the early 2030s and FID targeted for 2027; a second well (J-2) follows. (Upstream Online / Rigzone, 10 Jul 2026) [paywall] [link](https://www.upstreamonline.com/carbon-capture/inpex-starts-drilling-first-carbon-capture-appraisal-well-offshore-japan/2-1-2014545)
- **Fugro wins Saipem survey contract for UK's first offshore CO₂ pipeline (Northern Endurance)** — Fugro has been contracted by Saipem to provide integrated marine survey and inspection services for the Northern Endurance Partnership (NEP) — the bp/Equinor/TotalEnergies JV building the UK's first offshore CO₂ transport-and-storage system. The scope covers nearshore and offshore surveys, installation monitoring and third-party crossings along the 145-km offshore pipeline to subsea injection facilities in the Endurance saline aquifer (~1,000 m below the North Sea, up to ~1 Gt storage capacity). Storage is targeted to start in 2028. (Offshore Energy / OEDigital, 10 Jul 2026) [link](https://www.offshore-energy.biz/fugro-to-assist-saipem-with-pipeline-work-at-uks-first-offshore-ccs-project/)

## Markets & strategy

- **Gasunie-commissioned PwC study finds CCS the cheapest route to decarbonise Dutch industry** — A PwC study for Dutch gas-infrastructure operator Gasunie concludes the most cost-effective decarbonisation of Dutch industry runs through CCS, low-carbon hydrogen and hybrid electrification. CCS is modelled to abate ~21 Mt CO₂/yr (of which ~18 Mt via blue-hydrogen production), with steel ~9 Mt and hybrid boilers ~6 Mt — but the study warns that high energy costs, grid congestion and infrastructure timing are pushing companies to postpone investment. (Gasunie / ChemXplore, 10 Jul 2026) [link](https://www.gasunie.nl/en/news/pwc-study-cost-effective-decarbonisation-of-industry-mainly-through-ccs-hydrogen-and-hybrid-solutions)
