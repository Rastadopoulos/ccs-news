## Markets & strategy

- **JERA charters MOL and NYK ammonia carriers to ship CCS-enabled fuel from Louisiana to Japan** — JERA has booked two VLGCs from Mitsui OSK Lines and another two from NYK to move CCS-enabled low-carbon ammonia from the US$4 bn Blue Point project in Ascension Parish, Louisiana (CF Industries / JERA / Mitsui & Co.) to JERA's Hekinan thermal power station in Aichi from Japanese FY2029, anchoring the first dedicated trans-Pacific low-carbon ammonia supply chain built on US point-source CCS (~2.3 Mtpa CO₂ captured, ~1.4 Mtpa ammonia produced, >95% process-emissions abatement). (Splash247, 18 June 2026) [link](https://splash247.com/mol-and-nyk-seal-ammonia-carrier-charters-with-jera/)
