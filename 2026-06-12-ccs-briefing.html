EU Council and Parliament reach provisional agreement to strengthen ETS2 market stability reserve

Council of the EU · 11 Jun 2026 · [EU]

Negotiators agreed Wednesday night to roughly double the per-trigger release of the MSR for the new buildings-and-transport ETS — adding 20 million allowances on top of the existing 20 million release, with the mechanism now able to fire twice per year. The deal aims to shield households from carbon-price shocks ahead of ETS2 going live on 1 January 2028, and reinforces price predictability for downstream CCS-relevant carbon pricing signals. Member states must give priority to the social effects of ETS2 when spending auction revenues. The informal agreement still needs formal endorsement by Parliament and Council.
